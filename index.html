Tsinghua Birational Geometry Learning Seminar

This is a learning seminar on birational geometry at Tsinghua university.

We will focus on some special topics every semester.

This page will be updated as the seminar progresses.

If you are interested in participating or giving a talk, please contact Xiaowei Jiang.

Current Semester

Spring 2023 A: Birational Geometry Preprint Seminar

Time: Monday, 18:30-19:50
Location: Ningzhai W11

Description

This is a weekly reading seminar on recent (<2 years old) preprints in birational geometry. Each week, a speaker will choose any paper he or she likes to present.

Speakers are suggested to limit their talks to 60 minutes, leaving the remaining time free for other participants to discuss potential applications and further developments.
